Christian Williams Jire — Web Designer & Developer



After graduating in 2024 with a Bachelor’s Degree (First Class Honours) in Business Information Technology, I spent the last five years working both as a freelance web developer and designer and as part of a team in various companies across Nairobi, Kigali, London and California. I acquired project and time management skills, as well as the ability to communicate with team members and clients while effectively meeting milestones and deadlines.


As a freelance web developer and designer I collaborated with several graphic designers in California and London, at the same time maintaining clients in Germany, Ireland, Australia and the US. I have worked on a multitude of web and print based projects for a range of clients providing Web Design (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) and Development (Python, HTML, CSS, JS, jQuery, WordPress, Bootstrap, Responsive Layouts), eCommerce Solutions, Corporate Branding and Graphic Design.


During my BIT degree I studied Information Architecture, and therefore have a keen understanding of wireframes, sitemaps and user experience design which I put to good use on every project I work on. I am currently working in The UK between London and Manchester. I am always available to discuss your project over the phone or via zoom. These days I enjoy spending my free time reading novels, baking, painting, traveling, hiking, working out and playing the piano.
